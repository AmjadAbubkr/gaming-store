Game Technologie Account Deletion

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Delete your account in the app

Open the Game Technologie app on your Android or iOS device, sign in, go to the home screen, open Delete Account, and submit the deletion request.

What happens after the request

Your deletion request is recorded on your account profile and your session is ended immediately. The remaining account data is then removed through the store support workflow under the current backend rules.

Need help outside the app?

If you cannot access the app, contact the store through WhatsApp at +23565032926 and include the email address tied to your account.
