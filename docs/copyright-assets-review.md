# Copyrighted Asset Review

These app assets should be treated as replacement candidates before Google Play submission unless you own the rights or have explicit permission.

## Flagged assets in current active screens

- `assets/hero-spidey.png`
  - Used in:
    - `src/features/auth/screens/LoginScreen.tsx`
    - `src/features/auth/screens/RegisterScreen.tsx`
- `assets/home-hero-a.jpg`
  - Used in:

- `assets/home-hero-b.jpg`
  - Used in:

- `assets/consoles-hero.png`

- `assets/games-hero.png`

- `assets/ET_PS5Accessories_16.webp`
  - Used in:
    - `src/features/products/screens/AccessoriesScreen.tsx`

## Why they are risky

- They depict copyrighted characters, game art, or branded hardware marketing imagery.
- Google Play review can flag brand misuse, misleading affiliation, or unlicensed media use.

## Safer image sources

- Unsplash
- Pexels
- Pixabay
- OpenGameArt

Always review the license on the exact asset you choose, even on free-image sites.
