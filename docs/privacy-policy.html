Game Technologie Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Who this policy covers

This policy applies to the Game Technologie Android app published under the package com.cybernexus.gamingstore.

Data we collect

We collect the account and order information you provide in the app, including your name, email address, phone number, user identifier, and your order history.

Why we collect it

We use this information to create and authenticate customer accounts, display customer profiles, submit and track orders, support WhatsApp checkout, and help store administrators manage order fulfillment.

Where the data is stored

Account data is stored using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. Order handoff may involve WhatsApp when a customer chooses the WhatsApp checkout flow.
